What to Expect

Everything you need to know for a strong start to your EMBA journey.

🎒 What to Bring

Laptop, business casual attire for classes, comfortable clothes for bootcamp activities, any pre-reading materials shared by the program office.

🚌 Bootcamp Logistics

Transportation from Sasin, accommodation, and all meals are provided. Departure early Saturday morning, return late Monday afternoon.

🤝 Cohort Bonding

This experience is designed to help you bond with classmates, develop leadership skills, and engage in interactive workshops and team-building activities.

📋 Program Format

Evening classes (6–9:30 PM) on weekdays + full-day sessions on Saturdays. Expect a rigorous but rewarding schedule balancing work and study.
