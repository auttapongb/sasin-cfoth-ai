🔷 Key Frameworks

Core mental models and methodologies for this course:

RACE Framework (Smart Insights)

Reach → Act → Convert → Engage. Full-funnel customer lifecycle management for digital marketing. Build awareness (Reach), drive interaction (Act), generate sales (Convert), build loyalty (Engage). Measure at each stage.

SOSTAC® Planning (PR Smith)

Situation (where are we now?) → Objectives (where do we want to be?) → Strategy (how do we get there?) → Tactics (details of strategy) → Action (who does what, when?) → Control (measurement, KPIs, analytics). Structured digital marketing planning.

See-Think-Do-Care (Avinash Kaushik)

Match content to customer intent: See (largest addressable audience), Think (considering but not ready), Do (ready to buy), Care (existing customers). Each stage needs different content and channels.

Marketing 5.0 (Kotler, Kartajaya, Setiawan)

Technology for Humanity. Five generations: 1.0 Product-centric → 2.0 Customer-centric → 3.0 Human-centric → 4.0 Digital/Traditional → 5.0 Technology for Humanity. AI enables predictive, contextual, and augmented marketing.

7Ps Extended Marketing Mix

Product, Price, Place, Promotion + People, Process, Physical Evidence (Presence). The service-dominant logic upgrade of the classic 4Ps. Digital age adds the 8th P: Platform.
