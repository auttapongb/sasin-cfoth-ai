📖 Additional Readings

  1. Helmer, Hamilton — The 7 Powers of Business Strategy (2017)
    • 7 Powers: Scale Economies, Network Economies, Counter-Positioning, Switching Costs, Branding, Cornered Resource, Process Power
    • Key equation: NPV = M₀ × g × s × m
    • Benefit + Barrier framework for each power
    • Netflix (Scale), Intel (Scale vs. memory failure), Pixar (Cornered Resource)
  2. Schumacher, E.F. — Small is Beautiful: Economics As If People Mattered (1973)
  3. HBR — On Strategy, 10 Must Reads (2011)
  4. Porter, M.E. & Kramer, M.R. — "Strategy and Society: The Link Between Competitive Advantage and CSR" HBR Dec 2006
    • CSR should be integrated into strategy, not treated as separate
    • Inside-out vs. outside-in value chain analysis
    • Strategic CSR: where business and society intersect for shared value
  5. Prahalad, C.K. & Hammond, A. — "Serving the World's Poor, Profitably" HBR Sep 2002
    • 4 billion people earn <$2,000/year — vast untapped market
    • Business case for Bottom of the Pyramid (BoP) markets
    • Examples: village phones, microfinance, affordable products
