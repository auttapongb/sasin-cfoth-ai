Three Core Modules

Designed for the EMBA workflow — record in class, study smarter, ace your exams.

🎙️

Module 1: Lecture Recorder & Summarizer

Record lectures from phone/laptop — auto-transcribed via Whisper, summarized by AI. Extracts key concepts, frameworks, and professor's emphasis. Organizes everything by subject automatically.

🔨 Coming Week 5-6
⚡

Module 2: Realtime Lecture Assistant

Live transcription during class with real-time context analysis. Detects emphasis patterns, flags likely exam topics, cross-references readings. Generates predicted exam questions after each lecture.

📋 Planned Week 9-10
🧠

Module 3: AI Study Tools

RAG-powered chat for your course materials. Auto-generated flashcards with spaced repetition. Quiz generator (MCQ + case-based). "Explain Like I'm 5" mode. Progress dashboard with weak-area detection.

📋 Planned Week 7-8
